Cat

Cats are one of evolution's most charismatic creatures. They can live on the highest mountains and in the hottest deserts.

  • Class: Mammalia
  • Phylum: Chordata
  • Order: Carnivora
  • Family: Felidae

Cats are similar in anatomy to the other felids, with a strong, flexible body, quick reflexes, sharp retractable claws, and teeth adapted to killing small prey.
